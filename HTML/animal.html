Learn About Animals

Lions are known as the "King of the Jungle" because they are strong and powerful.

Giraffes are the tallest animals in the world! They have long necks and legs.

Cheetahs are the fastest animals on land! They can run incredibly fast, up to 60 miles per hour!

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth! They have big ears and long trunks.
